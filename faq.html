What happens if I don't have time to swipe all the cards from a month in one go?

I go to "Recently Deleted" and I can see the pictures there. Why?
Apple does not allow any app to fully delete pictures from your phone, they force us to send them to "Recently Viewed". But don't worry, they automatically get deleted after 30 days.
Can I recover any of the pictures I have deleted?
Yes! Once you allow deleting, they will end up in the "Recently Viewed" collection of your roll. From there you can recover any.
